GPA: 3.96/4.00, ranked 1st among all students in the major. I received my bachelor's degree from the College of Flexible Electronics, Shaanxi University of Science and Technology. Our team includes PhD students and postdoctoral researchers such as Prof. [Youyi Song](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=gSzHVNkAAAAJ), Dr. [Zhizhe Lin](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=j2aV91UAAAAJ), Postdoc. [Farooq Ahmed](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=bTVezlQAAAAJ), and Postdoc. [Shahid Ali](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=B3jovoIAAAAJ), who are actively contributing to their respective research areas. I have published more than 8 academic papers in IEEE TCE, IEEE IoTJ, IEEE SPL, IJCTA, and other top journals or conferences. My publications have been cited over 17 times, with an h-index of 3. In addition, I have obtained 2 software copyrights. I have led 1 Graduate Science and Technology Innovation Project and participated in 1 National Undergraduate Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program and 1 Provincial Graduate Innovation Research Project. I have received the National Scholarship, the National Second Prize in the 21st Huawei Cup China Postgraduate Mathematical Modeling Competition, the First Prize in the 14th National Undergraduate Mathematics Competition, and the Provincial Gold Award in the China International College Students' Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, among more than 10 awards and honors.

My research interest includes Spatiotemporal Data Mining, Time Series Analysis, Machine Learning, Intelligent Transportation System and Traffic Flow Forecasting.
